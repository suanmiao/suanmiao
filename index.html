About Me

I am a keen developer. I have developed over 10 applications and released to market 5 applications. My work is used by over 60,000 users and my open-source project gained over 200 stars on Github.

I am also a passionate researcher in mobile field, my co-authored paper been accepted by ICDCS 2015, and further research on mobile energy consumption is continuing.

I care about problems facing the mobile industry: energy consumption, information overload and information security.

More than that, I am eager to explore the future of mobile devices: wearable and IoT devices.(Click to see my recent IoT project)
